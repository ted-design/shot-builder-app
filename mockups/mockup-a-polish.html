What's different in Approach A

Time Ruler Gutter

A 48px mono-font time column anchors every card to a wall-clock time, replacing implicit ordering with explicit temporal context.

Connected Field Toggles

Checkboxes in the settings bar now wire to every card simultaneously. Toggling "Talent" collapses that row across all 8 shot cards instantly.

Semantic Icon Colors

Each metadata type has a distinct color: teal for products, indigo for talent, green for location, amber for notes. Scannable at a glance.

Left Color Bar

A 4px accent bar on the card left edge carries the shot's color tag without overwhelming the card. Provides quick visual grouping in a dense list.

Unscheduled Tray

Shots without a time slot surface below the timeline in a dashed-card grid. No hidden backlog — producers see the full shot list at once.

Banner Entries

Full-width gradient pill banners for shared events (load-in, lunch, wraps) span both tracks without consuming a card slot. Duration shown inline.
