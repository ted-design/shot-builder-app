Design Notes

  • Minimal color footprint — only a 2.5px left border carries category info. Body stays editorial-neutral.
  • Best fit for the near-black editorial design direction — color is restrained, informational, not decorative.
  • Sort order: priority → gender → media → other. Within each category, alphabetical.
  • Size: text-xxs (11px), rounded-md, px-2 py-0.5. Same compact footprint as current.
  • Dark mode: Border colors brighten slightly (400→400 level Tailwind colors). Body stays dark surface.
  • Implementation: Add border-left-color override per category. Everything else stays as-is.
  • Pros: Least disruptive change. Keeps editorial restraint. Easy to scan by border color.
  • Cons: Subtle — users may not immediately notice category grouping. Less visual pop than Option A/C.
