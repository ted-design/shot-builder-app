Design Comparison

Option A: Scrollable Timeline

  • + Familiar vertical scroll pattern
  • + NOW indicator shows temporal context at a glance
  • + Progressive disclosure via expandable sections
  • + Floating quick actions always accessible (thumb zone)
  • + Confirms attendance right in the hero card
  • - Long scroll to reach contacts or notes
  • - Heavier visual density in the schedule section

Option B: Card Stack

  • + Priority-ordered: most important info first
  • + Day progress bar gives at-a-glance context
  • + Tab bar gives direct access to all sections
  • + Bottom sheet for detail drilldown without losing context
  • + Contact grid is more scannable (2-col layout)
  • - Tab bar consumes vertical space
  • - Schedule is less detailed on the main card
