UX: Scene headers act as collapsible group dividers. The left accent color distinguishes scenes visually. Collapsed scenes show only the header with shot count. The context menu (three dots) provides Rename, Add Shot, Ungroup All, and Delete Scene.

Implementation: Scenes are stored as a scenes subcollection or as a sceneId field on each shot document. The "Sort: Scene" mode groups shots by scene, with ungrouped shots at the bottom.
