Design System Summary
  • Primary is near-black (#18181b) — every button, progress bar, and emphasis element
  • Immediate Red (#E31E24) reserved for: logo mark, sidebar active indicator, destructive actions, notification counts
  • Borders do separation — shadows are minimal (Notion/Linear style)
  • Typography is editorial — light weight (300) headings, tight tracking (-0.02em), Inter font
  • Neutral palette is zinc — cooler than slate, cleaner against white
  • radius-md (6px) for buttons/inputs, radius-lg (8px) for cards
  • No competing accent colors on buttons — near-black creates calm, editorial density
